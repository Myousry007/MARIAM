About Us

TriSun Commercial Brokerage LLC is a go-to point for all your financial services and needs where the customer’s needs are always put first. We are partnered with leading banks and financial institutions across the UAE which allows us to fulfill all your banking needs with ease and give you, the customer, peace of mind. TriSun is heavily involved in providing financial solutions, which extends to, but is not limited to, credit cards, personal financing and mortgage options from leading financial institutions in the UAE, which also helps us provide you with competitive rates! Our team is spearheaded by an individual having 25+ years of banking experience in the UAE market across all departments in retail banking, who has built the company on the grounds of putting the customer needs above all else by having them interact with individuals who have been trained to provide the top tier of financial services.
